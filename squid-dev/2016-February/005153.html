On 02/17/2016 10:16 AM, Dave Lewthwaite wrote:
> In the crtd daemon scenario is the daemon not responsible for passing
> back the full certificate chain required? Or is it specifically only
> responsible for the host certificate and key?
>

The crtd daemon does not send back full certificate chain.
Squid sends to the crtd daemon a CA certificate to be used to sign the 
generated certificate. And the daemon sends back the generated certificate.
