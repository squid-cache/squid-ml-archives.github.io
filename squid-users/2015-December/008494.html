A simple test, and quick fix. 

ping6 ipv6.google.com 
No reply. 
Add in your interfaces : 

auto 6to4
iface 6to4 inet6 6to4
        local YOUR_EXTERNAL_IP

ifup 6to4 

and ping6 ipv6.google.com again. 
Not the most elegant solution but works here for me.
