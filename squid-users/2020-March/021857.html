I would like to proxy chaining squid to parent proxy on the cloud, Netskope
proxy.

key=/etc/squid/ssl_cert/myCA9.pem generate-host-certification=on
dynamic_cert_mem_cache_size=4MB*

*acl step1 at_step SslBump1*
*ssl_bump peek step1*
*ssl_bump bump all*
