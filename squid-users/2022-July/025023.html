> Many things "work" in regex when they are not right.
>
> As Matus said the dot in the domain needs to be escaped:
>
>    ^.*adobe\.com$
>
>
> Also, the "^.*" in your pattern does nothing useful, it should be omitted.
>
> Leaving you with the pattern:
>
>     adobe\.com$
