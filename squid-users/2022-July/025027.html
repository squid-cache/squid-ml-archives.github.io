I would assume that if you want to match something like dstdomain you would use:
(^(.*\.)?)adobe\.com$
 
Or two regex:
\.adobe\.com$
^adobe\.com$
 
I like very much: https://rubular.com/
 
Which allows you to see visually the matches.
