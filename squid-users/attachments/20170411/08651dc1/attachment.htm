<br>
Place these lines above any "http_access allow" rules that you have:<br>
<br>
  acl expired src <a href="http://192.168.2.0/24" rel="noreferrer" target="_blank">192.168.2.0/24</a><br>
  acl expiredAllowed dstdomain mycompany.local<br>
  deny_info 302:<a href="http://mycompany.local/expired.htm" rel="noreferrer" target="_blank">http://mycompany.local/<wbr>expired.htm</a> expired<br>
  http_access deny !expiredAllowed expired<br>
