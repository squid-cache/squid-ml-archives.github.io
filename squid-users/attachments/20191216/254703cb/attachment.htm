    <p>That's not really a subject for this list - search online for

    <p>You use a syntax like "(.<a href="http://microsoft.com"
        rel="noreferrer noreferrer" target="_blank">microsoft.com</a>|.<a
        href="http://windows.com" rel="noreferrer noreferrer"
        target="_blank">windows.com|(</a>.<a

        rel="noreferrer noreferrer" target="_blank">windowsupdate.com/.*\.(cab|exe|ms[i|u|f]|[ap]sf|wm[v|a]|dat|zip)|foo.com)"</a></p>
    <p>eg (x|y|z(a|b)) would match x, y, za and zb.</p>
